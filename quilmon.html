Quilmon is a Cobblemon themed modpack of Minecraft (1.21.1), but there is a twist to the modpack. It replicates classic Minecraft from the alpha and beta era, and it also keeps the features of modern Minecraft. The modpack keep mostly the vanilla part, but it also adds some challenges, quality of life changes and some improvements as it keeps that same base Cobblemon experience.


Modpack features!

Take a look of some of the silly and cool features of the modpack! C:

The early and golden age look and feel of Minecraft.

- Gameplay, look and feel from the beta 1.7.3., but it keeps the modern feature of 1.21.1.
- Returning mobs, mechanics, progression and events to spice up your adventure.
- Quality of life features to help improve gameplay and builds.
- Revamped base Cobblemon experience with progression, level scaling and much more!
- Added some groovy bats (Also come in a very epic mega variant!)
- Expand your adventure with the classic legendary dimension mod: The Aether
- Optional resource pack to bring back the look and feel of Old Minecraft (Pre 1.14 texture, Beta and Alpha texture. Also betafied modern texture of mobs, blocks and items)
- Replaced Herobrine by Mudkip

Play with friend or random person you never met before!

The modpack will also come with an official SMP server where you can meet and play with other people, but the server is under a whitelist. To join the official server, you can join our Discord server and fill out a small form about why you should be accepted in the server. Once moderation check your form and accept it. You can directly join the server from the Multiplayer tabs in-game (The server are already in list).
