The official server rules!

When you join and play in the official quilmon server. You may aknowledge and respect every rules from this page.


Server rules

- Be respectful with other players. There no tolerance for any hate speech, slur and/or intention to start drama.
- Do not Spam in chat.
- No NSFW / NSFL topic in the chat.
- Any hacked client, cheating software, or other ways to break normal survival rules, are strictly not allowed. This includes: Xray, Flying, Kill Aura, Aim Bot, Increase Reach, etc...
- Griefing/Stealing from other people's items/building are prohibited.
- PVP is allowed, but the winner must give back all items to the defeated player.
- Traps/Pranks are allowed, but they cannot be excessive, and every item from deaths needs to be given back.
- AFK is allowed.
- Have fun and happy mining!


Immersive painting mod rules

This modpack use Immersive painting as side mod to allow people importing picture/art from internet link or from your PC to use it as painting. Some rules are also apply here.


- NSFW and pornography material are prohibited.
- NSFL imagery are also not allowed.
- Meme / jokes are allowed. But hateful, racism and any problematic image material is not allowed.
